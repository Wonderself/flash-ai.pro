Security

Security is the architecture.

How we protect your data — by design, not by promise.

Your data never leaves

The strongest security control is topology. A private LLM inside your perimeter means your documents, prompts and outputs never transit a third-party API. There is no provider to trust, because there is no provider in the loop.

Models are open-weight and auditable. Inference runs on your hardware. Updates are applied by us, inside your environment, under your supervision.

Israeli-grade practice, French-grade compliance

For hardened deployments we collaborate with specialised security firms from the Israeli ecosystem — penetration testing, network segmentation, monitoring. On the compliance side, our architectures are GDPR-native and designed with the EU AI Act in mind; for LATAM deployments, Argentina's Ley 25.326 and its EU adequacy status are built into the design.

Security review available as a standalone engagement — including a second opinion on an existing AI deployment.

LLM × CYBER

LLMs in cybersecurity: a paradigm shift in threat intelligence

Large language models are transforming how security teams see, triage and respond. The same architecture that answers your business questions can read millions of log lines, correlate indicators of compromise across sources, summarise a malware report in seconds and draft an incident response playbook adapted to your stack.

Deployed privately, this capability stays inside your perimeter: your threat data, your detections, your playbooks — never shared with an external API. It is the convergence of our two worlds: sovereign AI infrastructure and Israeli-grade security practice.
